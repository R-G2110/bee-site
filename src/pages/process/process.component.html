Our Honey Production Process

Discover the journey from hive to jar and our commitment to preserving honey's natural goodness.

1

Beekeeping

Our skilled beekeepers carefully tend to our hives, ensuring the health and happiness of our bees.

2

Harvesting

Frames are removed from the hives and honey is extracted using a gentle, cold-press method to preserve quality.

3

Filtering

We minimally filter our honey to remove only large particles, preserving all the beneficial pollen and enzymes.

4

Packaging

Our honey is carefully jarred in small batches and each jar is inspected for quality before being shipped to you.
